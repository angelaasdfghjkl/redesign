About

Sushi Village is a family-run restaurant in New York City. With our decades long expertise in the restaurant business, we pride ourselves in serving quality food and continuously hold ourselves to the highest standard. From sourcing local farm-to-table options to searching far and near for one of a kind flavors, we only use the freshest* ingredients. So come by and taste our signature sashimi, don buri, udon and bento boxes. We will be sure to satisfy your cravings and bring you the most delightful dining experience.

*Including fresh seafood directly flown from Japan through our partner, Tokyo Ichiban Foods
